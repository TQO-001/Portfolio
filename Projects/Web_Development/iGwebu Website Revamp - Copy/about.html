About
iGwebu Homecare

Our Story

Welcome to Igwebu Groups Pty Ltd, your trusted partner in premium detergent products. As a newly established company, we are dedicated to supplying, distributing, and selling top-quality detergents that cater to all your home care needs.

Our Commitment

Igwebu Groups Pty Ltd was born out of a passion for providing exceptional cleaning solutions that make a difference in every household. Our founders, driven by a vision of excellence and a commitment to quality, embarked on a journey to create a brand that stands out in the detergent industry. Through innovation, hard work, and a relentless pursuit of perfection, Igwebu is poised to quickly become a name synonymous with reliability and excellence.

Our Products

We take pride in our range of high-quality detergent products designed to meet your cleaning needs. Our flagship product, the Igwebu Dishwashing Liquid, is formulated to provide superior cleaning power while being gentle on your hands and the environment. Soon, we will be expanding our product line to include Igwebu Hand Washing Powder and other home care products, all crafted with the same dedication to excellence.

Why Choose Us?

  • High Quality: Our products are made from the finest ingredients to ensure outstanding performance.
  • Best Value for Money: We offer competitive prices without compromising on quality.
  • Environmental Care: Our eco-friendly products and sustainable practices reflect our commitment to preserving the environment.
  • Social Well-being: We believe in giving back to the community and promoting social well-being through our business initiatives.

Choose Igwebu Groups Pty Ltd for a cleaner, greener, and brighter home. Join us in our mission to redefine cleanliness with products that you can trust.

FAQs

Does iGwebu operate outside KwaZulu-Natal?

Currently, iGwebu is only available within KwaZulu-Natal. However, we are actively working on expanding to other provinces and eventually reaching international markets.

When will the full range of iGwebu Home-Care cleaning products be available?

The complete product line is scheduled for launch by February 2025, ensuring we cater to every household's cleaning needs.

Is iGwebu stocked in all major retail supermarkets?

At present, iGwebu products are available at select retailers, including Superspar (South Coast), Phoenix Cash & Carry, Tri Circle, Bellims, and certain petrol stations and tuck shops. We are continuously working to expand our retail presence.

Can individuals or businesses become iGwebu distributors?

Yes, we welcome local distributors and offer stock pricing for your convenience. Reach out to us for details on how to become a distributor in your area.

What makes iGwebu products unique?

iGwebu products are crafted with high-quality ingredients to ensure superior cleaning results. Our commitment to sustainability and affordability sets us apart in the market.

How can customers provide feedback on iGwebu products?

We value customer feedback. You can share your experience with us through our website's contact form or via email at info@igwebu.co.za.

Does iGwebu offer bulk purchasing options for businesses?

Yes, we provide bulk purchasing options for businesses, ensuring competitive pricing for larger orders.

Are there any plans for online sales or e-commerce availability?

Yes, we are working on launching an online store to make iGwebu products more accessible across South Africa.

Does iGwebu offer any promotions or discounts?

Periodically, we run promotional offers. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about the latest deals.
